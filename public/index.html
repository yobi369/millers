About PremiumGrain Mills

Established in 1973, PremiumGrain Mills has been at the forefront of grain processing innovation. We specialize in converting raw grains into premium flour products using cutting-edge milling technology and sustainable practices.

While we serve large commercial clients, we are now proud to offer our premium products directly to consumers and small businesses through our new online store.

With ISO 9001:2015 certification and HACCP compliance, we ensure the highest standards of food safety and quality control in every bag we ship.
